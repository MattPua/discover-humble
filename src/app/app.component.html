DiscoverHumble is my second timed product challenge of 2018. The Goal: redesign the HumbleBundle Library experience in 36 hours.


DiscoverHumble came about due to a problem I was having with having too many games in my HumbleBundle library. With so many games, it was difficult to figure out which game I wanted to play next.


DiscoverHumble is a way for people to re-discover their games on HumbleBundle in a different way.


Let me know your thoughts here.


Note: The items you currently see are items that are found in my library. Want to set up this up with your own HumbleBundle? Check out the instructions here.
